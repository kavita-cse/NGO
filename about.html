About Kaarn Social Welfare Foundation

Our Story

Kaarn Social Welfare Foundation began with a simple belief: small, consistent actions lead to monumental change. Founded by a group of passionate community members, our organization has grown into a driving force for awareness and responsibility. We realized that many of society's most pressing issues—from environmental degradation to civic apathy—stem from a lack of awareness. We set out to change that.

Today, SAKHI acts as a bridge, connecting resources, volunteers, and those in need. We work tirelessly to ensure that our communities are informed, empowered, and equipped to build a better future together.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To build an aware, responsible society that consistently makes conscious and sustainable choices, driving collective action toward a universally better future.

Mission

To systematically promote awareness on critical social and environmental issues, encouraging habits that empower every individual to take meaningful, everyday actions.

Core Values

  • Awareness

  • Responsibility

  • Compassion

  • Sustainability
