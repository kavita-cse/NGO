Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Welcome to Kaarn Social Welfare Foundation. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect the following information through our forms:

Contact Form Information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Subject
  • Message Content

Application / Volunteer Form Information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Area of Interest / Volunteering Role
  • Messages, Applications, or Additional Information You Provide

We do not require user registration or login accounts on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for purposes including:

  • Responding to inquiries and messages
  • Processing volunteer applications
  • Communicating regarding programs, initiatives, and opportunities
  • Improving our services and community outreach
  • Maintaining internal records related to foundation activities

3. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or loss. However, no internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared:

  • When legally required
  • To protect organizational rights and safety
  • With trusted service providers supporting website operations (if applicable)

5. Data Retention

We retain submitted information only for as long as necessary to respond to requests, manage volunteer applications, and fulfill operational and legal obligations.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to improve user experience and understand website performance. Users may disable cookies through their browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised update date.
