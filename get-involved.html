Volunteer With Us

Join our network of passionate individuals who are dedicated to creating a positive change. Whether you have a few hours a week or are looking for a full-time internship, there's a place for you at SAKHI.

Opportunities

Volunteer

Participate in weekend drives, awareness campaigns, and field activities. Perfect for those who want to contribute directly to the community.

Intern

Gain valuable experience in non-profit management, social media marketing, research, and event planning. Minimum 3 months commitment.

Community Participation

Become a local ambassador. Help us spread awareness in your immediate neighborhood, school, or workplace.
